Let \(A\) be a \(m \times n\) matrix. Suppose that \(\rref [A \mid I_m]\) has the form

\begin{equation*} \left[ \begin{array}{c|c} A_{0,0} \amp A_{0,1} \\ \hline 0_{r \times n} \amp A_{1,1} \end{array} \right] \end{equation*}

where \(A_{0,0}\) is full-rank (has no row of zeros). Then \(\col(A) = \ker(A_{1,1})\text{.}\)
