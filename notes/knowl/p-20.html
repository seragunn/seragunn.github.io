Let \(A\) be a \(m \times n\) matrix. Write

\begin{equation*} \rref [A \mid I_m] = \left[ \begin{array}{c|c} A_{0,0} \amp A_{0,1} \\ \hline 0_{r \times n} \amp A_{1,1} \end{array} \right] \text{ and } \rref [A^\top \mid I_n] = \left[ \begin{array}{c|c} B_{0,0} \amp B_{0,1} \\ \hline 0_{s \times m} \amp B_{1,1} \end{array} \right] \end{equation*}

where \(A_{0,0}\) is full-rank (has no row of zeros).
