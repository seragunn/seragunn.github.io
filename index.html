About me

I am a sixth year PhD student at Georgia Tech. I am interested in algebraic combinatorics and combinatorial algebraic geometry. Right now I am studying divisors on metric graphs and polynomials over hyperfields.

My advisor is Matt Baker.

I received my BMath from the University of Waterloo with majors in Pure Mathematics and Combinatorics & Optimization.

High School Math Day

Together with Wade Bloomquist and Evelyne Smith-Roberge, we are reshaping the old High School Math Competition to appeal to a broader audience. The new event will feature:

  • Board games and other fun investigative math activities (e.g. soap films)
  • A new logic based competition track focusing on more puzzle based math rather than algebra or geometry
  • Prizes for new competitors and creative solutions to open ended problems

More details coming soon! https://hsmc.gatech.edu/
